This repository contains teaching material for the image reconstruction in Pulseq

Topics
- Online Reconstruction based on ICE and Gadgetron and data labels in Pulseq
- Offline Reconstruction based on Matlab Pulseq image reconstruction scripts

The data label implementation is available in the https://github.com/pulseq/tutorials/tree/main/13_Labels_2DMode_Grappa

and Matlab reconstruction scipts are availabel in the https://github.com/pulseq/pulseq/tree/master/matlab/demoRecon
